Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Poets4Life Automation Application ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our services, specifically regarding our integration with the TikTok Developer API.

1. Information We Collect

Our App is a private automation workflow (powered by n8n) utilized solely for managing and publishing content to our official Poets4Life social media channels. We only collect and access:

  • Basic Profile Information: We access basic TikTok profile details (such as OpenID, Display Name, and Avatar) strictly for identity verification during the internal login and authorization process.
  • OAuth Authentication Tokens: Access tokens and refresh tokens provided securely by TikTok to authorize video publishing.
  • Content Data: Video files, captions, and scheduling details that are intentionally designated to be posted onto our own TikTok profile.

2. How We Use Information

We use the accessed information strictly for the following purposes:

  • To automatically upload and publish videos to our authorized TikTok account as configured in our backend pipeline.
  • To maintain and refresh authentication tokens securely to ensure uninterrupted automated posting services.

We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, profiling, or any other commercial usage outside of Content Publishing.

3. Data Storage and Security

All authentication credentials and tokens are encrypted and stored locally within our secure cloud infrastructure and private n8n database instance. No third party has access to these keys. We apply standard industry security protocols to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification of tokens.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We never sell, rent, trade, or share any user data or authentication tokens with third-party companies, advertising networks, or external entities. Data is only transmitted directly to TikTok's official endpoints during the automated video execution process.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We only retain authentication tokens for as long as the automation pipeline remains active. If we decide to deprecate the pipeline or delete the application integration from our TikTok account, all associated tokens, session files, and cached content logs will be permanently deleted from our servers immediately.

Data Deletion Request: If you wish to request the manual deletion of your stored authentication data from our databases, please contact us at contact@poets4life.com. Additionally, you can instantly revoke our application's access at any time via your TikTok Security Settings.
