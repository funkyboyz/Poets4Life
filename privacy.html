Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At Poets4Life Studio ("we," "our," or "the App"), your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our video scheduling and publishing services via our integration with TikTok.

1. Information We Collect

Our platform operates with minimal data collection. We only request necessary permissions (Scopes) via the TikTok API to provide our core functionality. We collect:

  • Basic Profile Information: Such as your TikTok display name and avatar, strictly to verify your identity during the secure authorization process.
  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth access and refresh tokens provided directly by TikTok to authorize video uploads and publishing on your behalf.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To securely upload and publish your scheduled video content to your authenticated TikTok account as requested by you.
  • To maintain active authentication tokens to ensure uninterrupted publishing services.

We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, profiling, or any other commercial usage outside of the core Content Publishing features.

3. Data Storage and Security

All authentication credentials and tokens are encrypted and stored locally within our secure cloud infrastructure. We apply standard industry security protocols to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification of tokens.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We never sell, rent, or share your user data, authentication tokens, or video content with third-party companies, advertising networks, or external entities. Data is only transmitted directly to TikTok's official endpoints during the automated video execution process.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We only retain authentication tokens for as long as you actively use our platform. If you decide to disconnect the App from your TikTok account, all associated tokens become immediately invalid and are permanently deleted from our servers.

Data Deletion Request: You can instantly revoke our application's access at any time via your personal TikTok Security Settings. If you wish to request the manual deletion of any residual connection data from our secure server, please contact us.

6. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, compliance reviews, or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our developer support team at: contact@poets4life.com.
